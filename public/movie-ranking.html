The Best Movies According to Avani

My top 3 movies of all-time

Crazy Rich Asians

A delightful rom-com that follows the hilarious and heartwarming journey of a young woman as she accompanies her boyfriend to Singapore to meet his incredibly wealthy family.

The Big Sick

Based on a true story, this charming rom-com revolves around an interracial couple dealing with cultural differences and unexpected challenges when one of them falls seriously ill.

To All the Boys I've Loved Before

A sweet and endearing story of a high school girl whose life takes a turn when her secret love letters to her crushes are accidentally sent out, leading to unexpected romances.
